
AST-101 Instructor Notes
Facilitation guidance

This lab is designed as a guided beginner lab. The instructor should focus on conceptual clarity before moving learners into live Asterisk configuration.

Key teaching points
A PBX routes calls between internal and external endpoints.
An extension is a dialable identifier.
A SIP endpoint is the registered device or softphone.
A dial plan is routing logic.
Unknown extensions must be handled deliberately.
Discussion questions
What happens operationally if two users are assigned the same extension?
Why should learner lab credentials be temporary?
What evidence proves that a learner understands the call flow?
Why should PBX systems be isolated during training?
Common learner mistakes
MistakeCoaching response
Confusing extension with endpointExplain that the extension is the number; the endpoint is the device or SIP identity.
Ignoring invalid numbersHighlight the security and user-experience risk of unhandled dial strings.
Writing configuration without validationReinforce test-driven lab evidence.
